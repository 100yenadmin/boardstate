## Ideas — "What should the vocabulary include?"

The design principle: we ship the vocabulary (trusted builtins + rails + templates),
you write the sentences. Missing a builtin kind? A binding source? A capability?
Make the case here — the bar is "load-bearing for many boards," not "possible."
